Description

The Rabbit Haul is a single-player cozy farming and tower defense game where you nurture your garden and upgrade your farm and abilities by day, and battle the mischievous Trash Panda Posse, a group of mean-spirited raccoons that want to rampage through your carefully cultivated garden and steal from your haul by night.

Grow Your Haul

Store harvested fruits and veggies in your haul to withstand gluttonous attacks from the raccoons at night. The more crops in your haul, the more that will be needed to take you down.

Prepare Your Defences

Grow powerful plant towers during the day to defend your garden against waves of enemies at night. Harvest your garden and use the currency you earn to upgrade weapons and towers.

Plan Your Garden

Strategically design your garden’s layout and select the right plants to grow. Each plant has unique abilities and synergies with other plants and wildlife.

Plan a Delicate Ecosystem

Nurture and cultivate your garden’s natural defences. Learn about synergies between plant towers and crops and use biodiversity to overcome the hordes of trash pandas. Leverage symbiotic relationships between other forms of wildlife and take part in nature's ability to collaborate and adapt.
